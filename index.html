The Countryside Code

"Take nothing but pictures, leave nothing but footprints, kill nothing but time"

It is important to respect our wildlife. The peak district is one of the UK's 15 national parks. These areas are protected by law to conserve and enhance their natural beauty, wildlife and cultural heritage, and to promote opportunities for the public to understand and enjoy their special qualities.

This vast span of rolling hills has not always been freely accessible by the public; for that, we must thank the members of the 1932 Kinder Scout mass trespass. Back when the farmlands were restricted by wealthy landowners, 500 walkers formed a protest by simply walking where they were forbidden. Now we can do so freely, we should honor their efforts and do so respectfully.
